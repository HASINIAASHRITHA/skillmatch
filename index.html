A comprehensive 6-week journey to transition from Computer Science to UX Design

  • 🎨 Adobe XD Tutorials - Adobe (Free)
  • 📱 Mobile App Design Course - Udemy ($49.99)

📅 6-Week Learning Plan

1

Week 1: UX Fundamentals

Introduction to UX design principles, user research basics, and design thinking methodology.

2

Week 2: Research & Analysis

User interviews, persona creation, competitive analysis, and problem identification.

3

Week 3: Information Architecture

Site mapping, user flows, wireframing, and content strategy fundamentals.

4

Week 4: Visual Design

Color theory, typography, layout principles, and creating style guides.

5

Week 5: Prototyping

Interactive prototypes, usability testing, and iteration based on feedback.

6

Week 6: Portfolio Development

Case study creation, portfolio website setup, and job application preparation.

💼 Project Ideas to Build Your Portfolio

  • E-commerce Mobile App Redesign - Pick a popular app and improve its user experience
  • Food Delivery Service Dashboard - Design an admin panel for restaurant owners
  • Educational Platform for Rural Students - Focus on accessibility and simple navigation
  • Personal Finance Tracker - Create intuitive budgeting and expense tracking interface
  • Healthcare Appointment Booking System - Design for elderly users with larger fonts and simple flows
  • Local Event Discovery App - Help users find events in their city with location-based features

About SkillMatch

Our Vision

To bridge the gap between traditional college education and modern industry requirements, empowering students from Tier 2 and 3 colleges to achieve their dream careers through personalized learning paths.

Our Mission

We believe every student deserves equal opportunities regardless of their college tier. Our AI-powered platform creates custom learning roadmaps, connecting academic knowledge with practical industry skills through curated resources, peer networks, and real-world projects.

Who We Serve

Our platform is specifically designed for students from Tier 2 and 3 colleges who are ambitious, motivated, and ready to take control of their career journey. We understand the unique challenges you face and provide the tools and community support needed to overcome them.
