About SkillMatch

SkillMatch bridges the gap between traditional college education and modern career requirements, specifically designed for students from Tier 2 and Tier 3 colleges who have big dreams but need the right guidance to achieve them.

Our Vision

To democratize career opportunities by making quality career guidance and skill development accessible to every student, regardless of their college tier or background.

Our Mission

Empower students with personalized roadmaps, practical skills, and real-world projects that make them job-ready and confident in their career journey.
